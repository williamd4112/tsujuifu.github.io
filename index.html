Pose Estimation with Human Segmentation Enhancement
We joint learn of pose estimation and human segmentation to give bottom-up pose model a body structure concept. The experimental result shows that it can reduce false-positive prediction effectively.

What are You Looking?
We combine object detection and gaze prediction to detect what people see in an image. Our two-phase model has a similar prediction accuracy as MIT's end-to-end one.

Wear Out
We modify GoogleLeNet's architecture to support multi-target recognition. Given a clothing, we recognize the color and category for both on and under. Our LeNet model with oversampling surpasses original SVM on DeepFashion dataset.
